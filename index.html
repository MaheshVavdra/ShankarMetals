PROFILE

Established in the year 1998, we SHANKAR METALS are one of the noted Retails, trader, distributor, exporter, wholesale and supplier of a broad selection of Steel Pipes, Plates and Fittings. Our products Corten Steel Plates, Carbon Steel Structural Plates, Corrosion Resistant Steel Plates and Stainless Steel Plates that are extensively appreciated and accepted for their rugged construction, longer operational life, ease of installation, high corrosion resistance and ease of maintenance.

At our company, to manage the compilation without hassles, we have hired a team that looks after the spacious warehouse and packaging unit that we have established. From the very beginning, we have followed the strategy of proffering the best quality possible, by examining the flawlessness of each product before it is delivered to the customers. Our sincere efforts and determination, we have earned a long list of clients in the market.

We are a sole proprietorship firm under the capable leadership of Mr. THANCHAND KUMAWAT. It is because of his regular guidance, consistent motivation and amazing industry experience that has inspired & helped us to attain the organizational goals. We stand as the preferred choice in the industry and wish to continue with the legacy in upcoming years.
